Principal Investigator

Sungbok Shin, Ph.D.  신성복

Assistant Professor

Sungbok Shin is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, College of Computing, at Sogang University. He is also affiliated with the dept. of Artificial Intelligence and the dept. of Applied Biomedical Engineering. Sungbok seeks to answer what makes learning effective for novices. To answer that question, he develops computational tools, systems, and methodologies. In particular, he has been working on helping visualization novices improve their chart designs.

He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Maryland, College Park (2024), advised by Dr. Niklas Elmqvist, with a dissertation entitled Simulation, Representation, and Automation: Human-Centered AI for Augmenting Visualization Design. He holds an M.Eng. and a B.S. from Korea University, where he worked with Dr. Jaegul Choo.

Prior to Sogang, he held postdoctoral appointments at Aarhus University (with Prof. Niklas Elmqvist), at Aviz@Inria Saclay (with Dr. Jean-Daniel Fekete), and as a visiting scholar at Seoul National University (with Prof. Jinwook Seo). His research interests span Human-Centered AI, Data Visualization, Human-Computer Interaction, Biomedical Informatics, and Situated Analytics in AR.
