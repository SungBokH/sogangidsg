Interactive Data Science Group

The Sogang Interactive Data Science Group is based at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering @ Sogang University, Seoul, South Korea. The group is led by Dr. Sungbok Shin. The group mainly studies topics such as Human-Centered AI, AI Governance, and Information Visualization. We build interactive systems that augment human activity through the use of Artificial Intelligence.

Brief Research Intro
  • Augmenting Visualization Reading + Authoring using AI Visualization is used as a tool to communicate insights effectively. In this project, the focus is on augmenting how people read and author visualizations. We build computational tools or systems that help lay audiences read and design them.
  • Interfaces for Augmenting Human Activities We study how interactive systems can help human activities. The goal is to help humans perform tasks more effectively and efficiently. This spans adaptive dashboards, conversational interfaces, and situated analytics in augmented reality.
  • Other AI-Driven Applications Occasionally, the group ventures into other AI-driven domains, from recommender systems to biomedical informatics, wherever an interesting problem invites us.

A Note to Prospective Students

If you are interested in pursuing research in the topics above, please feel free to reach out at  sbshin90@sogang.ac.kr.

HCI and Data Visualization are inherently interdisciplinary, and hence we welcome applicants from a range of backgrounds. Students with Computer Science background are encouraged, but other majors (e.g., Psychology) might potentially be a good fit.

I don't yet have concrete plans to recruit students, but I might. I am always looking for motivated and smart students! So if you are interested, please reach out!

Please include a brief statement of interest, your CV, and any work you are proud of.
